Use Proxies to Bypass

cbgamesdev.github.io

Main site for tons of unblocked games. Can be glitchy with Tsunami but still works well.

Minecraft 1.5.2

Classic port of Minecraft. Opens in about:blank tab. May take a moment to load.

coolmathgames.com

Legendary game site. Many games deprecated post-Flash, but still some gems.

geo-fs.com

Browser-based flight simulator. Works but may lag on slower devices.

games-unblockedd

Massive collection of games. Functional, but cbgames is generally better.

Spelunky

Classic indie platformer. Controls are on-page. Wait for full load.

yolk.quest

Shell Shockers mirror. Choose “stomp” mode to play.

Stumble Guys (Browser)

Works well with Ultra-Max. Full game experience in browser.
