Tsunami

Proxy

Tsunami is a website that uses a proxy to remotely access any website. All websites that you visit through Tsunami will be unblocked. It's a little complicated, so here is a tutorial on how to use it:

Browsing

Determine whether you want to search Google or go to a specific URL.
If searching, select Ultraviolet; if going to a specific site, select Stomp.
Enter the URL or search query in the bar and press Enter.
To reload the page, click the blue reload button in the top right.
To exit, press the blue X in the top right.

Arcade

To navigate to the arcade, click the arcade button in the top right.
It's recommended to use Webretro for emulators.
Otherwise, just click the game you want to play and enjoy.
