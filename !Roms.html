Romsgames

Romsgames is the website that I use to download the ROMs for the retro games that I want to use.

Using romsgames:

Determine the game that you want to play. Open Tsunami and go to romsgames.net. Search for the game in the top left of the page. Select the game you want. Click "Save Game" on the left side of your screen. Then follow the directions for Webretro.
